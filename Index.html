Introducing to Teasing Master Takagi-san

Introduction of the story

In a middle school, a boy named Nishikata is always be teased by his deskmate Takagi. He tries his best to strike back and tease Takagi. As time goes, Nishikata realize his strong emotion towards her.

Characters

Takagi

Main character of this anime. She is one of the top students in class. She is smart and cute.
Hobbies: Take a walk and comics
Weakness: armpit and likes to play tricks on him when she sees him
She feels in love with Nishikata gradually when she first met him in the beginning of the school semester because he found her lost tissue.

Nishikata

Main character of this anime. He doesn't have a good score in class.
He loves cats and dogs
Favourite comic: 100% Unrequited Love
He always believes that Takagi doesn't love him and just want to play tricks on him. He always thinks that he can play tricks on Takagi, but finally he is tricked by Takagi.
